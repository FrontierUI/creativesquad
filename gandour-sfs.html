gandour sfs

At the Gandour Saudi Food Show 2025, we designed and executed one of the top creative booths at the SFS 2024–25, transforming the space into a destination rather than just a stopover. The concept was developed to reflect Gandour’s heritage and brand energy while creating an immersive environment that invited visitors to engage, explore, and stay longer.

Through impactful visual design, thoughtfully planned product displays, and interactive experience zones, the booth encouraged meaningful visitor interaction beyond a traditional exhibition visit. Strategic layout planning and experiential elements ensured smooth visitor flow while maintaining high engagement levels. The result was a standout presence that attracted over 20,000+ attendees, successfully converting footfall into memorable brand experiences and reinforcing Gandour’s strong positioning within the Saudi food industry.
